High Achievers 2021

The Science School students shine in 2021 O Level Q! A tremendous performance by one and all! Science School shines in Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Computer Science

MashAllah ! The Science School shines once again with a glittering performance by our students in CAIE Oct / Nov 2021 Exam Series of AS Level and O Level.

Congratulations to each and everyone! Students, Staff, Parents and Families! Especial congratulations to curriculum managers and teachers, the pride of TSS!

AS Level CAIE Result Percentages Oct / Nov 2021​

43 of 47 students take top grades in Mathematics! 96% A and B!
40 of 47 score top grades in Physics! 96 % with A and B!
31 of 45 score top grades in Chemistry! 80% A and B!
81% take A and B in Computer Science!

Islamiat: A*& A, 82%, A*, A & B 96%
